CWL AT IC DELTA was first organized at IC Delta in January 22, 1946. The group of ladies were led by Mrs. Alice Hone as the Council President with Ms. Rose Beaubien as Secretary, under the Spiritual guidance of Fr. John Haurahan. The present membership is grateful to our sisters who courageously carried on the tradition through the years.
